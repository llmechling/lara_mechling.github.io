What is the general trend of the data?¶

The data shows a negative correlation between weight of the vehicle and the amount of miles that vehicle can drive per gallon of gas.

Would the slope be positive or negative?¶

The slope of the line would be negative because as the weight the miles per gallon decreases.

Interpret the meaning of the slope being -0.05¶

If the slope of the line is -0.05 this means that the equation of the line would be y = -0.05x, or that as y changes, x would change at a rate of -0.05 lbs (if lbs is the measure of weight)
